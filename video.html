Show Hosting/Reporting

One of the most fun production experiences I've had was creating and reporting for "NYUMM," a culinary adventure to some of New York's best eateries. On this excursion for #HashtagNYU, I hosted and reported on a trip to John's Pizzeria in the West Village of New York City for the segment NYum which I created with videographer Sandra Tan.

On this episode of NYum we explore the East Village's Pomme Frites restaurant with two NYU students.

During high school I was an active member of "Central Intelligence" our school's broadcast journalism program. During my tenure we were ranked the #1 broadcast journalism program in the country, and my partner and I were the recipients of three Mid-America Student Emmy awards. Through this program I was able to pair my love of storytelling, production, and personal interests such as this inside look into "Girls on the Run."
