Welcome!

My name is Valerie, and I am a graduate of the University of Miami where I studied Visual Journalism and Marketing. I have worked at a number of non-profit, production, design and marketing places both in the US and abroad. I am constantly striving to pursue work that combines my interests in social good, business, design, travel, and storytelling (both visual and verbial) to improve the world. I hope this site gives you a brief glimpse into my life and experience. I'd love to hear from you! Before you go exploring, here are a few fun facts about me: I lived on a 1,000 passenger ship for 4 months traveling to 16 different countries in 2014, I have 22 aunts and uncles, and my passion for women's issues has led me to touch nearly 20,000 people globally.
